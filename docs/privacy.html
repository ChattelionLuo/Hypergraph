Privacy

Privacy notice.

This static website is designed to minimize data collection while presenting research, product concepts, and demonstration probabilities.

Information we may receive

If you email us through a contact link, we may receive your email address, name, organization, and the content of your message. Static hosting providers may also collect ordinary technical logs such as IP address, browser type, and request time.

How information is used

We use contact information to respond to inquiries, research collaborations, and commercial discussions. We do not use this website to operate betting accounts, gambling accounts, wagering wallets, or bookmaker services.

No gambling service

Project Hypergraph does not collect information for gambling participation and does not provide betting strategy, wagering advice, or gambling facilitation. Public probability displays are informational and research-oriented.

Retention and contact

We keep communications only as long as needed for legitimate correspondence. For privacy questions, contact contact@projecthypergraph.com.
